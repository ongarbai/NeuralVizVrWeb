About NeuralVizVR

NeuralVizVR is an innovative, open-source platform developed at Maynooth University, funded by the SFI Frontiers for the Future Program (MAIVV, 2022–2025). Our mission is to improve neural network interpretability and analysis by leveraging immersive 3D visualization, interactive exploration, and advanced AI-assisted techniques, making complex models more accessible and understandable to a wide range of users.

Central Research Question

    How can immersive 3D visualization, interaction, and AI-assisted analysis enhance the interpretability, accessibility, and validation of neural networks across diverse modalities and applications?

Objectives

  • Develop a Multi-Modal Platform: Create a platform accessible via web, desktop, and VR modes, ensuring usability across expertise levels and hardware configurations.
  • Enhance Visualization and Interaction: Provide tools for constructing, modifying, and analyzing neural networks as 3D representations, enabling real-time interaction and experimentation.
  • Integrate Formal Verification Methods: Validate model robustness, reliability, and adherence to criteria through formal verification methods.
  • Integrate AI Assistance: Enhance LLM capabilities for advanced model interpretation, personalized guidance, and intuitive support across expertise levels.
  • Develop Autonomous Systems Simulation: Introduce a virtual autonomous vehicle simulation for real-world-inspired scenarios for data collection, model training, and testing.

Current Progress

Our existing platform enables users to visualize and analyze neural networks in an immersive 3D environment, accessible in both VR and desktop modes. Key features include:

  • Visualization: Immersive exploration of neural network layers, activations, and data flow in 3D.
  • Interaction: Construct, modify, and analyze networks in real time through intuitive 3D interactions.
  • Interpretability Tools: Provide insights into feature influences on predictions.
  • Robustness Analysis: Test model resilience to various attack types.
  • LLM-based AI Assistance: Offers basic real-time explanations and guidance for interpreting simple inputs.

Future Plans

Building on the current platform, we aim to extend NeuralVizVR's capabilities to further enhance its functionality, accessibility, and user experience. Planned developments include:

  • Multi-Modal Platform: Expand accessibility by developing web and desktop-compatible versions alongside VR, with enhanced options for customization.
  • Formal Verification Integration: Incorporate formal verification methods to ensure model safety, reliability, and compliance with defined criteria.
  • Enhanced AI Assistance: Advance LLM integration to provide deeper model interpretations, personalized guidance, and richer user interactions.
  • Autonomous Systems Simulation: Introduce a virtual autonomous vehicle simulation environment for realistic data collection, model training, and testing in dynamic scenarios.
  • Comprehensive Evaluation: Conduct iterative usability assessments to evaluate cognitive load, user satisfaction, and overall platform effectiveness across diverse participants.
